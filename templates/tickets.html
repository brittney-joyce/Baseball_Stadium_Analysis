Ticket Prices


Let's take a dive into ticketing pricing across the MLB


  • The major dip in 2011 attendance may be due to the economic recession that hit the United States. The rise in ticket pricing in 2013 can be potentially due to the Dodgers overall on field performance. Ticket sales were the highest in 2017, as this was the year the Dodgers played in the World Series.
  • The trend in Dodgers ticket pricing seems to go hand in hand with how the economy is performing. Overall MLB prices have been fairly consistent with a slight raise in ticket prices. Due to the amount of success the Dodgers experienced in their run to the World Series, we see a spike in pricing compared to the rest of the league.
